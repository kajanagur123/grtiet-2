IoT Class

**Internet of Things (IoT)** refers to a network of interconnected devices that collect and exchange data over the internet. These devices—such as sensors, appliances, and wearables—can monitor, control, and automate tasks in real-time without human intervention. IoT is widely used in smart homes, healthcare, agriculture, industry, and transportation to improve efficiency, safety, and convenience.

ROBOTICS CLASS

**Robotics** is a field of engineering and science that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots. Robots are programmable machines capable of carrying out tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. Robotics combines elements of mechanical engineering, electronics, computer science, and artificial intelligence. It is used in various sectors like manufacturing, healthcare, space exploration, military, and everyday life to perform tasks that are repetitive, dangerous, or require precision.
